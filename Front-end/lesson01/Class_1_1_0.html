**Good morning, everyone.** It’s my great honor to introduce the **7th Nanshan Artificial Intelligence Forum**, held on October 11, 2025, at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications. This forum brought together more than **300 AI researchers**, with a focus on cutting-edge fields like **brain-inspired computing** and **multimodal models**. Under the theme *“Face-to-Face with National AI Experts,”* we were joined by five leading scholars, including **Prof. Pan Gang** and **Prof. Yan Tianyi**. Our objectives were clear: First, to **share expert knowledge** and promote the national “AI+” initiative, especially in **AI+Health** and **AI+Manufacturing**. Second, to **foster collaboration**—resulting in joint training programs with top universities and regional partnerships across Sichuan and Chongqing. Third, to **tackle social challenges**, such as aging and uneven medical resources, with real AI-driven solutions.
